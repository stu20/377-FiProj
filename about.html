How To Use This Page

Step 1: Enter Search Criteria

Start by entering your search criteria in the provided form fields. Specify the course name or ID number as required.

Step 2: View Results

After entering your search criteria, click the "Filter" button or hit "enter" key to retrieve the relevant course information. The results will be displayed on the page.

Step 3: Filter and Sort Results

You can continue to sort through the available array and a node graph will appear based on the level of courses found in your search result.
